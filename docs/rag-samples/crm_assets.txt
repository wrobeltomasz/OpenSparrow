CRM Assets — Asset Registry
==============================
Assets track company-owned physical and intangible property.

FIELDS:
- asset_tag: Unique barcode/inventory tag (e.g. AST-00001)
- name: Asset description
- category: Hardware / Vehicle / Real Estate / Equipment / Software License / Other
- purchase_date: Acquisition date
- purchase_price: Original cost
- current_value: Book value after depreciation
- depreciation_method: Straight Line / Declining / None
- assigned_contact_id: Responsible person (optional)
- location: Physical or logical location
- warranty_end: Warranty expiry date
- next_inspection_date: Scheduled maintenance/inspection
- status: Active / Maintenance / Retired / Lost
- notes: Condition notes, insurance claims, history

DEPRECIATION METHODS:
- Straight Line: equal amount reduced per year
- Declining: higher depreciation early, lower later (common for vehicles, equipment)
- None: no depreciation applied (e.g. real estate valued at market)

ASSET STATUSES:
- Active: in use and operational
- Maintenance: under repair or scheduled service
- Retired: decommissioned, no longer in use
- Lost: missing, theft or loss reported (file insurance claim in notes)

CALENDAR:
- warranty_end dates appear on calendar (grey, 14 days advance warning)
- Use next_inspection_date to schedule physical reviews

VIEWS:
- Menu → Assets Summary: count and total book value by category
- Dashboard widget "Active Assets" counts all records with status = Active

TIPS:
- Filter warranty_end < today to find expired warranties needing renewal
- Filter status = Maintenance to monitor equipment under repair
- Record insurance claim reference numbers in the notes field for Lost assets
- Use assigned_contact_id to track who is responsible for each asset
