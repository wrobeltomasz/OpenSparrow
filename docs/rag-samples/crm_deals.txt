CRM Deals — Sales Pipeline
===========================
A Deal represents a sales opportunity with a specific company and contact.

FIELDS:
- title: Name of the deal (e.g. "Enterprise License Q2")
- value: Estimated revenue in currency units
- stage: Current pipeline position
- expected_close: Target closing date
- company_id: Linked company
- contact_id: Primary contact person

DEAL STAGES (in order):
1. Lead — Initial interest, not yet qualified
2. Qualified — Confirmed need and budget
3. Proposal — Quote or proposal has been prepared/sent
4. Negotiation — Terms being finalized
5. Won — Deal closed successfully
6. Lost — Deal did not proceed

HOW TO PROGRESS A DEAL:
- Move stage manually in the grid or edit form, or drag its card between lanes on the
  Deals Board (Kanban view, Menu → Deals Board) — dragging a card updates the deal's
  stage the same way as editing the field directly
- Log activities (calls, meetings, emails) to track communication
- Attach quotes under the Quotes subtable
- Once a quote is marked Accepted, a draft invoice is generated automatically (see
  "Automations" below) — no manual invoice creation needed

DEALS BOARD (Kanban):
Menu → Deals Board shows one lane per stage (To Do-style columns: Lead, Qualified,
Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost). Each card shows the deal title, linked company, value
and expected close date. Dragging a card to another lane moves the deal to that stage.

AUTOMATIONS ON DEALS:
- "Deal Won — Close Notification": when a deal's stage is set to Won, the user who made
  the change gets an in-app notification linking back to the deal.
- "Quote Accepted — Generate Draft Invoice" (see CRM Quotes and Invoices doc): when a
  quote linked to a deal is marked Accepted, a Draft invoice is created automatically,
  copying the quote's amounts and linking it back to both the deal and the quote.

RELATED RECORDS:
- Activities: scheduled calls, meetings, tasks linked to this deal
- Quotes: pricing offers for this deal
- Invoices: payment requests for this deal

PIPELINE VIEW:
Go to Menu → Pipeline Summary for a count and value breakdown by stage.
Deals dashboard widget "Deals by Stage" shows a bar chart.

CALENDAR:
Deals appear on calendar by expected_close date (yellow, 3 days advance warning).
