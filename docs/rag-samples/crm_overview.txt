CRM Demo — OpenSparrow
======================
Customer Relationship Management system for tracking companies, contacts, deals, and sales activities.

TABLES:
- companies: Client organizations. Fields: name, industry, website, phone, email.
- contacts: Individual people at companies. Fields: first_name, last_name, email, phone, position, company_id.
- deals: Sales opportunities. Fields: title, value, stage, expected_close, company_id, contact_id.
- activities: Actions on deals (calls, meetings, emails). Fields: type, notes, scheduled_at, done.
- leads: Incoming prospects not yet converted. Fields: source, first_name, last_name, company_name, status.
- products: Catalog of sellable items and services. Fields: sku, name, unit_price, category, active.
- quotes: Pricing offers sent to clients. Fields: quote_number, status, subtotal, tax, total, valid_until.
- invoices: Payment requests. Fields: invoice_number, status, issue_date, due_date, amount_total, paid_at.
- assets: Company-owned physical and intangible assets. Fields: asset_tag, name, category, current_value, status.

RELATIONSHIPS:
- Contact belongs to Company (company_id)
- Deal belongs to Company and Contact
- Activity belongs to Deal and Contact
- Quote belongs to Deal
- Invoice belongs to Deal and optionally a Quote
- Asset can be assigned to a Contact (assigned_contact_id)
- Product to Contact is many-to-many via product_contacts table

WORKFLOWS:
1. New CRM Deal: Add Company → Add Contact → Create Deal → Log Activity
2. Convert Lead: Capture Lead → Add Company → Add Contact → Create Deal → Send Quote

VIEWS (read-only summaries):
- Pipeline Summary: deal count and value by stage
- Leads Funnel: lead count by status
- Revenue Summary: invoice count and total by status
- Assets Summary: asset count and book value by category
- Revenue by Period: invoices drilled down by year → month
