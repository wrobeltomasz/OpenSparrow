CRM Quotes and Invoices
=========================

QUOTES
------
A Quote is a priced offer sent to a client for a specific Deal.

Fields:
- quote_number: Unique identifier (e.g. Q-2026-001)
- status: Draft → Sent → Accepted / Rejected / Expired
- valid_until: Offer expiry date
- subtotal: Sum before tax
- tax: Tax amount (e.g. 23% VAT)
- total: Grand total
- notes: Internal or client-facing comments

Quote lifecycle:
1. Draft — being prepared
2. Sent — delivered to client
3. Accepted — client agreed (triggers invoice creation)
4. Rejected — client declined
5. Expired — valid_until date passed without response

Calendar: Quotes appear on calendar by valid_until date (purple, 7 days advance warning).
Dashboard widget "Quotes by Status" shows a bar chart.

INVOICES
--------
An Invoice is a payment request. Can be created from a Quote or directly from a Deal.

Fields:
- invoice_number: Unique identifier (e.g. INV-2026-005)
- status: Draft → Sent → Paid / Overdue / Cancelled
- issue_date: Date invoice was issued
- due_date: Payment deadline
- amount_net: Net amount before tax
- amount_tax: Tax charged
- amount_total: Total payable
- paid_at: Timestamp of payment (NULL if unpaid)
- notes: Internal notes

Invoice statuses:
- Draft: not yet sent
- Sent: delivered, awaiting payment
- Paid: payment received (paid_at is set)
- Overdue: due_date passed without payment
- Cancelled: voided

Calendar: Invoices appear on calendar by due_date (red, 7 days advance warning).
Dashboard: "Outstanding Invoices" widget counts Sent + Overdue invoices.
Revenue Summary view shows invoice count and total by status.
Revenue by Period view drills down by year → month with totals.
