CRM Leads — Prospect Management
=================================
A Lead is an incoming prospect who has not yet been converted into a Contact.

FIELDS:
- source: How the lead was acquired (Web, Referral, Cold Call, Event, Ads, Other)
- first_name, last_name: Lead's name
- email, phone: Contact details
- company_name: Free text company (not yet linked to companies table)
- status: Qualification stage
- converted_contact_id: Filled when lead is converted to a Contact

LEAD STATUSES:
1. New — Just captured, no action taken
2. Contacted — Initial outreach made
3. Qualified — Need and budget confirmed, ready for conversion
4. Lost — Did not proceed

CONVERSION WORKFLOW (Convert Lead):
Step 1: Capture Lead — fill lead record with source and contact info
Step 2: Add Company — create company record for their organization
Step 3: Add Contact — create contact linked to that company
Step 4: Create Deal — open a deal for the opportunity
Step 5: Send Quote — attach a quote to the deal

LEAD SOURCES:
- Web: form submission or inbound inquiry
- Referral: introduced by existing client or partner
- Cold Call: outbound sales call
- Event: trade show, conference, or webinar
- Ads: paid campaign (PPC, social)
- Other: anything not classified above

LEADS FUNNEL VIEW:
Menu → Leads Funnel shows lead count per status.
Dashboard widget "Lead Sources" shows a pie chart by source.
Dashboard widget "Active Leads" counts all leads where status is not Lost.

AUTOMATIONS ON LEADS:
- "New Web Lead — Auto Contact": any new lead captured with source = Web is automatically
  marked status = Contacted, since a web form submission already counts as first contact.
- "New Lead — Welcome Email": any new lead that has an email address automatically
  receives a "Thank you for your interest" welcome email from the sales team.

DATA RETENTION (GDPR ANONYMIZATION):
Stale leads are subject to automatic anonymization: any lead older than 365 days (by
created_at) has its email, phone, first name and last name replaced with placeholder
values. This runs on a manual/scheduled basis from Admin → Anonymization — use
Preview (dry run) there to see which leads would be affected before running it.
